Clothing

From Digimon Masters Online Wiki - DMO Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Clothing are equipments that can be added to your Tamer to increase stats. Some clothing have slots for Attribute items to be inserted, increasing the effects of certain stats. Some clothing also changes the appearance of your Tamer. Clothing can be bought from NPCs, found as drops from enemy Digimon, given away as part of events, as prizes from the Capsule machines or purchased in the Cash Shop.

  • Digivices with options function in the same manner as Rings, Necklaces, Earrings, and Bracelets. The available options for Digivices include Digimon Attributes and Digimon Elements.
  • Digivice of Beginning Lv 2 has the maximum number of 7% in Digimon Attributes and 10% in Digimon Elements.
  • OT Digivice, Digivice of Adventure Lv 2, and all version of True Digivice has the maximum number of 10% in Digimon Attributes and 15% in Digimon Elements.
  • The crest effect in all version of True Digivice has no gameplay benefit. They only add a special aura visual effect to your tamer.
