KindSeal Item
ClassRare item Item

Right-click to use Seal Opener to be activated so as to get the buff according to the number of the seal while hunting.


  • Seals are items dropped by certain Digimon that can be used in Seal Master System.
  • Seal ability applies to the summoned digimon as passive stats.
  • Digimon seals have various abilities by type, increasing status in many ways.
  • Seals increase normal attack damage, defense, block rate, evasion, hit rate, critical, HP and DS.
  • The more various seals you collect, and the more levels you raise, the more various and stronger abilities your digimon will gain permanently.

Seal Opener - Can open the Seal Card on Seal Master System UI.
Seal Closer - Can make the Seal Cards tradable again.
- Both are obtained from Cash Shop, Server Reward, events or buying from another player.

  • Amount of Seal Card & Seal Opener required.
